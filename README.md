# economic_inequality
Economic Inequality is a repo focused on analyzing economic metrics to visualize economic inequality.

## Metrics (1900-Present)
Wealth Distribution - Measured by GINI Coefficient, inequality shrinking until 1973
Political Polarization - How many times were laws passed by bipartisan vote and signed by a president from the opposite party
Social Mobility - Percentage of people by class, ability to move from lower to middle class
Life Expectancy - General life expectancy
Hours Worked Per Week - General hours worked per week
Global Social Mobility - Ability of workers to gain wealth from work globally highest 1948-1973
College Tuition - Median college tuition in US, ramping up since 1973
Average Pay - Average pay per worker (Focus on jobs around 1983)
Top Tax Bracket Rate - General tax brackets
Home Building Rate - General housing inventory (both rate and aggregate count)
Unemployment Rate - General unemployment rate
